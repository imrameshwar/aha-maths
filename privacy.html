Privacy

Short version: Aha Maths has no accounts, no login, and almost everything below stays on your own device. We built it this way on purpose — a maths site for 5-year-olds shouldn't need a data-collection policy longer than the lessons themselves.

What lives on your device

The site uses your browser's localStorage — a small store that never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else. It holds:

1
Language & theme — your EN/हिंदी choice and your Dark/Light/Kid/High-contrast/Color-safe pick.
2
Accessibility prefs — text size, dyslexia-friendly font, reduced-motion setting.
3
Sound on/off — whether narration and sound effects are muted.
4
Lesson & story progress — which beats and lessons you've completed, so "Continue" and badges work.
5
A first name, only if you type one — the Story of Numbers lets you optionally enter a name so the characters can greet you. It's optional, stored only on your device with your progress, and never sent anywhere.

You can clear all of it any time via your browser's site settings ("Clear site data" / "Clear cookies and site data") — the site will simply forget your preferences and progress and start fresh, exactly like a first visit.

The email signup

If you choose to submit your email for lesson updates, it's sent to our form provider (currently Formspree) solely to email you about new lessons. We don't sell it, and you can unsubscribe any time. Nothing is submitted unless you fill in and press that form yourself.

Analytics

We may use a privacy-first analytics tool (no cookies, no cross-site tracking, no personal data — just aggregate counts like "how many people started this lesson") to see which lessons are working and which need fixing. If and when this is switched on, it will be named here.

Third-party video

Where a lesson embeds a YouTube Short, YouTube's own privacy policy applies to that embedded player once you interact with it — we don't control that, only Google does.

Children

Aha Maths is made to be used by children, alongside a parent, teacher, or guardian. We never ask for ages, photos, or contact details. The only optional input is a first name for the Story of Numbers greeting (see point 5 above) — it stays on your device and is never collected, sent, or seen by us.
