The Birth of Zero

A proof of the “accuracy sandwich.” Three layers are composited: a cinematic mood backdrop (where a Higgsfield clip drops in), the code-accurate math on top (every digit is rendered by code, never by AI), and bilingual text. Toggle each layer to see how it works.

① AI mood layer · Higgsfield drop-in

How this becomes “ultra” in production

① Mood → Higgsfield

The backdrop here is a coded stand-in. In production it’s replaced by a Higgsfield Soul/DoP clip (clay tablets, cosmos) — carrying mood only, never a number or word.

② Math → code

The place-value columns and the zero-drop are rendered by code, so “105” is always correct. AI never touches digits, symbols, or geometry.

③ Text → HTML

Captions are localisable EN/हिं overlays — never baked into pixels (AI mangles Devanagari). Remotion composites ②+③ over ①’s clip and exports the MP4.

Weight-gated for production: short loop, poster frame, AV1/H.265, reduced-motion + Save-Data aware. See PREMIUM-DESIGN-PLAN.md · §12.
